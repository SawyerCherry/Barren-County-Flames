Who We Are

Our mission is provide an opportunity for adults and children with developmental and intellectual disabilities the means to participate in a variety of athletic activities. Through the power of sports, we hope to provide our athletes the opportunity to develop physical fitness, develop a passion, and become an inspiration for individuals with disabilities and their community.

As a non-profit organization, we are entirely funded by contributions.
